Gothenburg Startup Hack

Sponsorship opportunities

A hackathon

The Gothenburg Startup Hack is about igniting the startup community, celebrating creativity and opening up the world of tech to more people. We want our city to be a place where startups thrive and create the next wave of innovation-based companies.

How it works

The 100 participants will get 12 hours to create something. They work in teams (max five people) and can build whatever they want as long as it can stand on its own. We really want to encourage people to make something out of that idea they’ve been saving for later, rather than building something related to their job or studies. Of course, the most important part is that it’s fun and inspiring.

This is the second edition of the Gothenburg Startup Hack, successful Startup Hacks have been held in Stockholm several times.

Why sponsor?

Connect your brand with the best minds in the Gothenburg tech community

Supporting Gothenburg Startup Hack will put your company in front of students, entrepreneurs, designers, hackers and startups that are eager to connect with the latest opportunities and trends. Sponsors of the event will inevitably increase their exposure to the digital and creative community.

Identify and recruit talent

Gothenburg Startup Hack is a great place to reach out to a wide audience of people from the local community in Gothenburg. It’s the perfect setup to find new, innovative talent in an environment where they actually demonstrate live how good they are at creating value.

Support a good cause

Connect with startups, hackers, makers and creators in the digital space as a supporter of Gothenburg’s tech initiatives and play an active role in promoting the local tech community.
