During the Day

The hack is open to anyone creative, who loves to build using technology.

We imagine a meeting place for students, designers, hackers and startup people. We expect you to provide a fun idea and go at it with great passion. For all this we will make sure you get fed and cared for during the day.

Getting tired of hacking? We've got distractions! Getting tired of your friends? We've got new ones!

At the end of the day, we all demo our prototypes to each other. The goal is to build something functional.

Also, code!

During the Night

All great days end with a great party. Have a beer with your new found friends. Below chandeliers and between bank boxes we shake our posteriors until we forget about tomorrow!

In order to attend, just "attend" the event at Facebook so we know you'll come. The party is for everyone, not just people attending the hack. See you there!

Visit event page at Facebook

Practical info

Please leave your finished products at home. The implementation must be done during the hack ('cuz this is where you hack).

There will be a small judge providing business and technical feedback on your prototype.

Application deadline is April 13:th
