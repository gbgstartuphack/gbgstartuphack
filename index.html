This hackathon is about igniting the startup community, celebrating creativity and opening up the world of tech to more people.

Also, code. ❤

The Hack

  • The Hack
  • The Party

During the Day

The hack is open to anyone creative, who loves to build using technology.

We imagine a meeting place for students, designers, hackers and startup people. We expect you to provide a fun idea and have at it with great passion. For all this we will make sure you get fed and cared for during the day.

Getting tired of hacking? We got distractions! Getting tired of your friends, we got new ones!

At the end of the day, we all demo our prototypes to each other. The goal is to build something functional.

Also, code!

During the Night

All great days end with a great party. Have a beer with your new found friends. Below chandeliers and between bank boxes we shake our posteriors until we forget about tomorrow!

The Location

Why a bank vault?

Haven't we all dreamt about hacking a bank? Let's make it possible for a day.

Get ready, deposit your valuables and start hacking on the 3rd of May!
